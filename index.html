ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?



In the hope of achieving youthful and fruitful India we have developed a website aiming to promote the concept of foster care.
In our website NGO's as well as families wanting to volunteer foster homes can register and take a step forward in building the bright future of orphans.
Funds and donations are also welcome to serve the orphan youth of India so,they are provided with all necessities.

OUR MISSION


This mission is for improving the quality of life of orphanns in India. We aim to give them the most beautiful gift of life in the form of a meaningful and loving family with whom these loving children can make beautiful memories.
This may help in significant development of our underpriviledged youth who can play a crucial role in achieving long term goal of developed India.
Our goal is to unlock the huge potential of orphans by providing them with a positive family environment which can empower them with resources and opportunities.
